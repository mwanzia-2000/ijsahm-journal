Manuscript Submission

Important Note: Manuscript formatting as per journal format is not mandatory during initial submission for review.

Normal Processing

Standard peer review process with typical turnaround of 4-6 weeks.

Send manuscript to:

ijsahm.normal@hscr-watch.org

Include your name, affiliation, and manuscript title in the email

Fast-Track Processing

Expedited review with decision within 2-3 weeks (additional fee applies).

Send manuscript to:

ijsahm.fast@hscr-watch.org

Mention "FAST TRACK" in the subject line

Before You Submit

  • Ensure your manuscript is original and not under consideration elsewhere
  • All authors have approved the submission
  • You have read and understood the publication fees
  • Your manuscript follows ethical guidelines (no plagiarism, proper citations)
  • You have prepared a cover letter explaining the significance of your work
