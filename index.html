International Journal of Strategic Management, Accounting and Human Resources Management (IJSAHM)

The International Journal of Strategic Management, Accounting and Human Resources Management (IJSAHM) is an internationally recognized, high-impact scholarly journal that advances knowledge, innovation, and best practices in Business and management disciplines. The journal provides a leading platform for interdisciplinary research, contributing to sustainable development, organizational excellence, and evidence-based decision-making across both developed and emerging economies.
