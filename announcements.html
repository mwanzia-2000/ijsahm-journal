Announcements

April 15, 2026

Call for Papers - Volume 2, Issue 1

We are now accepting submissions for the upcoming issue (Volume 2, Issue 1). Topics of interest include strategic management in post-pandemic economies, AI in HRM, sustainable accounting practices, and emerging market case studies. Submit your research before May 30, 2026 for priority consideration.

Special issue editors: Prof. John Mwangi (University of Nairobi) and Dr. Sarah Chen (National University of Singapore)

March 1, 2026

IJSAHM Now Indexed in DOAJ

We are pleased to announce that IJSAHM has been accepted for indexing in the Directory of Open Access Journals (DOAJ). This milestone reflects our commitment to open access publishing and high editorial standards. Authors can now enjoy increased visibility and discoverability of their published work.

January 10, 2026

New Editorial Board Members Announced

Welcome to our new editorial board members from 12 countries, strengthening our global reach and expertise. The new members include distinguished professors from Harvard Business School, London School of Economics, University of Cape Town, and University of Melbourne.

December 5, 2025

Best Paper Award 2025

Congratulations to Dr. Amina Hassan and team for winning the IJSAHM Best Paper Award 2025 for their article "Digital Transformation and Strategic Agility in African SMEs." The award includes a cash prize of $500 and a certificate of recognition.

October 15, 2025

New Partnership Announcement

IJSAHM has entered into a partnership with the African Management Development Institutes' Network (AMDI-Net) to promote management research across Africa. Joint special issues and collaborative workshops are being planned for 2026.
